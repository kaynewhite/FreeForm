Tome of Lore

Hotkeys, spellcraft, and the Architect's hand

Movement & sight

  • WASD — walk · Shift — sprint
  • Mouse — face the cursor (snaps to four cardinal facings)
  • Mouse wheel — channel Output (1–100%) for casting
  • Esc — close any open panel · returns focus to the world

Combat & spellcraft

  • 1 — equip basic attack (your racial weapon, or bare hands)
  • 2–6 — equip a quick-pick spell from the hotbar
  • Hold F — reveal the Spell Wheel (15 deeper-shelf spells)
  • Left-click — fire equipped spell at 20% tap-cast (cheap, weak)
  • Right-click hold — charge to your current Output%, release to cast
  • With basic attack equipped, click swings toward the cursor

Voice & codices

  • T — speak · Enter — send · Esc — leave chat
  • C Vessel · I Satchel · K Spellbook · J Quests
  • M Atlas · H Tome (this codex)
Encyclopedia of the Realm

I · The Five Races

  • Human — balanced vessel; modest control, modest resistance, the most flexible class.
  • Orc — high HP and stamina; brutal in melee, slow at the cast.
  • Elf — high mana cap and cast speed; fragile and easily winded.
  • Crystalline — high resistance; their bodies refract incoming damage at the cost of speed.
  • Voidborn — innate efficiency; spells cost less mana, but their bodies are brittle.

II · Vitals

  • HP — life. Reaches zero and the vessel is spent; the realm is permadeath.
  • Mana — fuel for spells. Regenerates slowly while you breathe.
  • Stamina — drained by sprinting and basic attacks. Catch your breath if it runs dry.

III · Output dial

  • Scroll the wheel to set Output between 1% and 100%.
  • Right-click hold charges to the dial; left-click is a fixed 20% tap.
  • Mana cost and damage both scale with Output — bigger bolts cost real mana.

IV · Spells (the eternal twenty-one)

  • Mana Bolt — violet/gold projectile. Reach 8, width 0.55, base 50 dmg @ 50 mana.
  • Quick-pick slots 2–6 hold five spells at the ready.
  • The Spell Wheel (hold F) reveals 15 more slots in a radial fan.
  • Empty slots are inscribed via the Spellbook — the Architect grants new spell forms.

V · Weapons of the realm

  • Free Hand — Architect's bare-fist channel; arc swing, no stamina cost.
  • Dagger — short reach, fast cadence, low stamina drain.
  • Club — wide arc, heavy thump, slow recovery.
  • Bow — long reach single-shot; aim toward the cursor.
  • Slingshot — quick low-damage projectile; cheap on stamina.
  • Katana — sweeping arc, the highest base damage of the racial weapons.

VI · Fellowship

  • Party — temporary bond between souls in the same shard. Open the Fellowship dropdown to invite.
  • Guild — long-lived order with a shared roster, banner, and chat channel.
  • Use /party <text> or /guild <text> to speak in those private channels.

VII · Death & the void

  • The realm is permadeath. When HP reaches zero, the vessel is gone forever.
  • You'll be returned to the forge to weave a new soul. Names and deeds live on in the chronicle.

VIII · Etiquette of the shard

  • Speak kindly. The Architect watches.
  • Right-click never casts on a friend; targeting respects party & guild bonds.
  • Whisper with /whisper <name> <msg> when the world is loud.
